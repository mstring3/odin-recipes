Lasagna Recipe!

How to make great homemade lasagna!

In this recipes, I'll be taking everyone back to a bit of the homestead with this famous recipes from back home in Yukarslavingard!

In the days of the gear wars and the abomitable snowman crisis, families didn"t have much to feed themselves and had to rely on Yukardur meat to get by! This recipes includes a bit of an old school tase with a new age feel!

Ingredients:

  • Moon Milk : 1 Cup
  • Yukardur Meat, Ground : 1 lb
  • Fairy Cottacge Cheese : One Village
  • Lava Sauce : Add to Desired Consistency!
  • Lasagna Sheets, Cooked

Steps:

  • Pre-Heat Oven to 1000 Degrees
  • Combine Ingrediants in Large Bowl
  • Layer cooked sheets and ingrediants to size of baking container
  • Bake Until Blackened and Smoking!
  • Enjoy with a nice glass of cold gasoline!
