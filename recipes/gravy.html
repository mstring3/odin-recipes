Time for Gravy!

This here is one of our finest homestyle recipes. This will send all the ladies to bed and all the cows to pasture! Buckle up brother!

Ingrediants:

  • Mars Dusted Flour : 1 Cup
  • Buttery Moon Milk : 3 Cups
  • Hagemendanor Butter : 1 Stick
  • Pristine Ground Space Sausage : 1lb

Steps:

  1. Git your skillet nice and warm with 1/4 of your butter, then throw the sausge in the brown to brown!
  2. Add your spices to your taste, cook sausge too desired doneness.
  3. When the sausage is done, add in the rest of your better and flour. Make a little rue and make sure that flower gets cooked! About 3 minutes.
  4. When thats good and cooking, add in your milk slowly by little and stir to your desired thickness. Gravy will thicken as it cools down!
  5. Get your friends and family and get bloated!
